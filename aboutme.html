about me


I am Nick.
A graphic designer and artist. I have a broad and changing practice with interdisciplinary work. Some of my recent focuses have been in accessible infrastructure and public art (All Aboard.) I graduated from the University of Connecticut with a BFA in Graphic Design where I gained a range of skills. Print, motion, web, and social media design were my main concentrations but I also have learned experience in fine art, exhibition, and installation practices.

Take a look at some of my other pages to see what else I have been up to.

Open for commissions.
